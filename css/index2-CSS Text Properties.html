PlayStation

PlayStation (プレイステーション, Pureisutēshon ?, officially abbreviated PS ) is a series of video game consoles created and developed by Sony Computer Entertainment , spanning the fifth , sixth , seventh , eighth , and ninth generations of video games . The brand was first launched on December 3 , 1994 in Japan . [ 1 ] It has a total of eight consoles — two of which are portable and a "Pro" version —, a media center , an online service , a line of controllers and a palmtop , as well as several specialized magazines

PlayStation

It was released on December 3, 1994, in Japan and on September 29, 1995, in the United States. Since its launch until 2006 (when its production was extinguished), PlayStation has sold over 103 million units.

Playstation 2

The PlayStation 2 (PS2) is the second video game console produced by Sony Computer Entertainment , after the original PlayStation. It was initially released on March 4, 2000 in Japan, on October 26 in North America, and later on November 24 in Europe. The PlayStation 2 is now the best-selling video game console in history: more than 150 million units have been sold worldwide.

Playstation 3

The PlayStation 3 (PS3) is the third video game console produced by Sony Computer Entertainment and the successor to the PlayStation 2 . It was released on November 12, 2006 in Japan, November 17 in North America, and March 29, 2007 in most of Europe and Oceania. In Brazil it was officially launched on August 11, 2010. [ 4 ]

Playstation 4

The PlayStation 4 (PS4) is a console produced by Sony. It came after the PlayStation 3 as a home console and after the PlayStation Vita in Sony's overall console lineup. The launch day of the console was November 15, 2013 in the United States and November 29, 2013 in Latin America (including Brazil) and Europe, according to data released by Sony itself. [ 5 ] The price of the console is US$399.00 or something around R$1000.00 in the United States of America, while in Brazil the price reached R$3999.00 at launch.

PlayStation 5
